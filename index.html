ICAAC Inteligent Cognetive Ability Assesment Tool

Our Project

The Intelligent Cognitive Abilities Assessment Tool is Sri Lanka's first automated cognitive assessment tool for Sri Lankan children.

Our solution includes a speech recognition system to evaluate child's verbal responses and vocabulary responses in Sinhala language, a Sinhala handwritten letter recognition model, hand drawn shape recognition model, gaze estimation, blink count detection and facial expression recognition model to evaluate the above four aspects of cognitive development of a child.

Attention Leve evaluation

Evaluating a child's attention span is important because it can indicate whether they are able to focus and pay attention in school or other activities. It can also help identify any potential learning disabilities or attention disorders that may need to be addressed. In addition, evaluating a child's attention span can provide insight into their overall cognitive functioning and help inform educational and behavioral interventions.

Commercialization

The cognitive evaluation tool that has been used is a very specialized tool that is not intended for use by the general population. The instrument is intended for use in Sri Lanka's district level hospitals' pediatric units.
